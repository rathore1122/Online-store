    About

    Onpricei.com is the ideal place to satiate all of your Indian food cravings and transports you into the quintessential home made food. It is known across the bhopal for its awesome food, great place and delivery services.

    The experience starts from the students who are persuaing their graduation from bhopal, away from their family and home, they didn’t get food that taste like their mother’s food. So, its give an idea to bring delicious food that not only taste like home made food but also made up of 100%natural and healthy ingredients.

    Onpricei.com’s dinning area with DJ and some interesting interior games play a vital role in enhancing the aesthetics of the restaurant. The kitchen of the restaurant resembles a dhaba with simmering effects of tandoors and steamy tawa preparations which makes your dining experience a memorable one.

    Onpricei was established with a aim to provide super-delicious food to everyone living in bhopal at an affordable price. And to make this true, We have an expert and experienced chef team who is good at delivering delicious foods, snacks, juices and other food items.
